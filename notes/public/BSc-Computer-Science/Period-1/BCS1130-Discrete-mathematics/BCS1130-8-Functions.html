Composition of functions §

Definition

Consider functions g:A→B and f:B→C Then (f∘g)(x)=f(g(x)) is the function obtained first using g and then using f

  • Domain of (f∘g) → domain of g
  • Codomain of (f∘g) → codomain of f
  • This condition must be true for the compound function: range(g)⊆domain(f)
